The Future of Localisation, Is Echolocation

Echolocation: The New GPS

Using groundbreaking, cutting edge SO-NR™ enabled technology, your TouchMi 4 MAX EDGE emits ultrasonic, high frequency sonic pulses and reads their reverberation signals in order to create a truly accurate virtual 3D image of your surroundings. Your smartphone is no longer blind, but can see, much like how a bat can.

31 long-range and short-range microphones ensure you're always within earshot and never not being listened to.

SO-NR™ enabled software creates an accurate 3D virtual image of your surroundings, so your phone knows exactly where you are.

Inspired by, tested and developed with bats.

The Future Of SO-NR ™ Echolocation Software

Echolocation technology in smartphones faces a bright future, diverse in both it's current application and its future theoretical application. Thanks to SO-NR™ enabled hardware and open source software, the software developer community to free to play a key role in shaping the potential of this innovative technology.

Using the 3D mapping capabilities of your TouchMi and the information harvested from thousands of other TouchMi users, you can navigate in the supermarket, the airport, the underground and even your home!
